How to Play

  1. Open Host Screen on a TV or laptop
  2. Set up teams and number of players
  3. Click "Team A QR" — each Team A player scans it on their phone
  4. Click "Team B QR" — each Team B player scans it on their phone
  5. Race to buzz in when a question appears!
  6. If correct: sit yourself on your couch (closer to winning) or pull an opponent back from theirs
  7. First team with all players on their couch wins! 🏆
