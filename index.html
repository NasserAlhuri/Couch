How to Play

  1. Host opens the host screen and configures Pusher
  2. Host sets team names and player count
  3. Players scan the QR code or open the link on their phone
  4. Enter your name to get your buzzer
  5. Race to buzz when a question appears!
  6. Correct answer: sit on your couch or pull an opponent back
  7. First team fully on their couch wins! 🏆
